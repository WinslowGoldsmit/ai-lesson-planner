# Quiz Generator Review v1

## Test Details

- Subject: Physics
- Class: 11
- Chapter: Motion in a Plane
- Topic: Scalars and Vectors

## What worked well?

- The quiz had a good balance of conceptual and numerical questions.
- The difficulty level was appropriate for Class 11 students.
- The questions covered the fundamental concepts of the topic.

## What needs improvement?

- Include more higher-order thinking questions that require students to connect multiple concepts.
- Include a few definition- or terminology-based questions for basic concept checking.

## Were the questions concept-based?

- Yes. Most questions tested conceptual understanding rather than simple memorization.

## Were any questions repeated?

- No questions were repeated.
- However, several questions followed a similar pattern. Some variety in question style would make the assessment more effective.

## Was the difficulty appropriate?

- Yes. The progression of difficulty was suitable for students encountering the topic for the first time.

## Improvements for Version 2

- Tag each question with its corresponding Bloom's Taxonomy level (Remember, Understand, Apply, Analyze, Evaluate).
- Arrange questions in increasing cognitive difficulty.
- Include a better mix of conceptual, numerical, and terminology-based questions.
- Show teachers which learning objectives and concepts each question assesses so they can quickly identify student learning gaps.
