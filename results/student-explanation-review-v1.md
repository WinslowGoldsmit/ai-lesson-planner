## Evaluation

### 1. What worked well?

* The explanation was clear, well-structured, and presented at multiple levels of detail, making the concept easier to understand.

### 2. What was confusing or unclear?

* The memory trick was not very memorable or intuitive. It could be simpler, more creative, and easier for students to recall during revision.

### 3. Which section was the most useful?

* The **Common Misconception** section was the most valuable. It went beyond simply explaining the concept by addressing mistakes that students are likely to make, leading to a deeper and more complete understanding.

### 4. Which section needs improvement?

* The **Memory Trick** section needs improvement to provide stronger, more practical mnemonics that students can remember easily.

### 5. Would a struggling Class 11 student understand this?

**Answer:** Yes

**Reason:** The explanation used simple language, logical sequencing, and relatable examples, making it accessible for most struggling Class 11 students.
